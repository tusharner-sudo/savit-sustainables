About Us

Promoting Sustainability &
the Rural Economy

Every product begins with the hands of our skilled farmers and local artisans — from harvesting bamboo and areca nut leaves to precision crafting in workshops.

Our Story

Savit Sustainables was born from a simple belief: that the products we use every day should not come at the cost of our planet. Founded in Nashik, Maharashtra, we work directly with local farmers and artisans to create beautiful, functional alternatives to single-use plastics.

Our name "Savit" draws from the Sanskrit root meaning "nurturer" — reflecting our commitment to nurturing both the environment and the rural communities we work with.

From bamboo drinkware and jute bags to compostable packaging and corporate eco-kits, every product we make carries the story of the hands that crafted it and the earth that grew it.
